# Comparison Operators
- Comparison operators work for **numbers**, **dates** and other **comparable data types**
  - Equal to (**==**), works also for **strings**
  - Not equal to (**!=**)
  - Greater than (**>**)
  - Greater than or equal to (**>=**)
  - Less than (**<**)
  - Less than or equal to (**<=**)
